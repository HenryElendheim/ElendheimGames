# Open questions

Still undecided. Good to revisit with the user. When one is settled, move it to
`decisions.md` with a one-line rationale.

- **Her current Norwegian level?** (total beginner / some basics / intermediate)
  — drives starting vocab and how fast scaffolding fades.
- **Bokmål or Nynorsk?** — recommend **Bokmål** (most common, Oslo, what most
  learners use) unless there's a regional reason for Nynorsk.
- **Personalize as her, or neutral character?** — naming it after her / an avatar
  that looks like her / the in-game letters being "from you" would make it feel
  like a gift made *for her*. Or keep neutral so she projects onto it.
- **Setting flavor?** — coastal fishing village (fjords, boats, gulls) /
  cozy mountain-forest town (snow, cabins, hytte life) / small city neighborhood
  (cafés, trams, shops). Each gives a different vocab world and look.
- **Scope of the first build?** — full hub life-sim with car + cards inside, vs a
  smaller life sim first (home + town + dialogue) that we expand later.
- **Engine specifics** — confirm Phaser vs plain canvas; decide on tilemap tool,
  asset style/source (commission, asset packs, AI-assisted, hand-drawn).
- **Backend?** — there's an existing Supabase setup in the main repo; decide
  whether the Norwegian game needs any backend (saves/sync) or stays local-only.
